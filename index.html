Our Introduction

EndoSurvivors International Foundation strives to raise awareness about endometriosis in Nigeria through education, advocacy and research. Engaged in a radical campaign to educate both the medical community and public, ESIF is committed to reducing the infamous 9-year delay to diagnosis. Adolescents, women and men alike ought to know that “severe menstrual pain is not normal” and should be taken seriously. The emphasis is on the critical importance of early diagnosis, timely intervention while simultaneously providing education to adolescents who are the next generation leaders in health care and education.
